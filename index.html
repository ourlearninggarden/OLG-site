Our Programs

Two rooms, rooted in the Living Garden Approach.

From first steps to kindergarten-ready, our Sprouts and Blooms rooms meet each child exactly where they are — with primary caregiving, responsive educators, and a real garden to grow in.

Sprouts Room · 18–36 months

Sprouts Room

Gentle, unhurried days where caregiving is the curriculum. A consistent primary Garden Keeper helps each toddler feel safe enough to explore and brave enough to try.

  • Primary caregiving — one consistent, familiar educator
  • Watering, harvesting & digging in our garden
  • “Heart songs,” sensory bins, water & messy art
  • Photos, naps & meals shared via Brightwheel
Full-time · Mon–Fri, 8:00am–4:30pm Join Waitlist
Blooms Room · 3–5 years

Blooms Room

Big questions and bigger imaginations. Through inquiry, gardening, yoga and music in the garden, our preschoolers build confidence and skills meant to last forever.

  • Inquiry-led learning that follows the child
  • Gardening projects, science & discovery
  • Yoga, music & dancing in the garden
  • Full-time & part-time preschool options

Our Philosophy

Different flowers make a garden thrive.

Our Learning Garden is a space where everyone belongs. Just as different plants and creatures make a garden grow, different ideas, cultures, languages and learning styles make our community stronger. We call this our Living Garden Approach — learning that is responsive, evolving, and deeply connected to each unique child.

Relationships come first

We believe strong relationships are the foundation of learning, with community at the heart of our days.

Caregiving is the curriculum

Children thrive when they are known, understood, and cared for as individuals. Through responsive relationships, we support each child's unique path of growth and learning.

Rooted in place

On the unceded territory of the W̱SÁNEĆ peoples, we learn alongside the land, each other, and the wider community around us.

Meet the Team

Meet our Garden Keepers.

Warm, qualified educators who tend our garden every day. They're the consistent, caring hands that help your little one feel safe enough to explore and brave enough to bloom.

Teacher Majda

Director & Sprouts Educator

An ECE with a Special Needs Certification, Majda co-founded Our Learning Garden on the belief that relationships are at the heart of early learning. She brings warmth, creativity and a love for the everyday moments of childhood.

Teacher Elena

Manager & Blooms Room Lead

An ECE with a Special Needs Certification, Elena brings energy, confidence and thoughtful leadership. She believes strong relationships help children grow, and loves weaving music, movement and yoga into joyful days.

Teacher Sarah

Blooms Educator

Sarah brings enthusiasm, kindness and a hands-on approach to every day. She creates engaging experiences that build confidence and a love of learning, helping every child feel welcomed and ready to explore.

Teacher Sabrina

Blooms Educator

With several years in early learning, Sabrina brings wisdom, confidence and consistency. She values strong relationships with families, offering the guidance and structure that help children feel secure, supported and capable. 

Teacher Grace

Infant Toddler Educator

As an Infant Toddler Educator, Grace is passionate about supporting children through their earliest years of growth. She creates nurturing spaces where infants and toddlers feel safe and connected, celebrating the small moments that make the toddler years so special.

🌱 Every educator is a certified Early Childhood Educator, first-aid trained, and chosen for warmth above all.
